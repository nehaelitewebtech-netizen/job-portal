Job description

Headquartered in Melbourne, Australia, we’re a completely online company with an ecosystem of sites and services to help people get creative. In September 2014, one of our sites,ThemeForest, was the 90th most visited website in the world and it is currently the most visited Australian-owned website on the planet! We have over 250 staff globally, and have paid out more than $224 million to our community of creative sellers. Recently, JobAdvisor awarded us the title of Australia’s Coolest Tech Company. On Envato Market thousands of people earn a living selling things like WordPress themes, photos, music tracks and illustrations. On our education platform, Tuts+, over 5 million people learn skills including code, illustration, photography, web design, and more, each month. We also run a freelance marketplace called Envato Studio. You might be surprised to know that not only do we run some of the biggest websites in the world; we’re also growing really fast!
