HOW IT WORKS

Creating an effective online job website involves several key components, both for job seekers and employers. For job seekers, the process typically starts with registration and profile creation, where they provide personal information, educational background, work experience, skills, and a profile picture. They can also upload their resume or use a resume builder tool provided by the website. The job search functionality allows them to use filters such as job title, location, industry, and salary range to find relevant job listings, while personalized job recommendations are provided based on their profile and search history. Applying for jobs is made easy through the "Apply" button on job listings, and they can track the status of their applications in their account dashboard. Additional resources such as articles on resume writing, interview tips, career advice, webinars, and workshops are available to enhance their job search skills.
